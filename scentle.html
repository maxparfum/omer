How to Play

Guess the fragrance in 6 tries. After each guess, you'll see 7 tiles showing how close your guess was:

The 7 Tiles:

  1. Brand - Exact brand match
  2. Country - Country of origin
  3. Style - Primary style/accord
  4. Note - Dominant note family
  5. Fresh/Sweet - Position on fresh-to-sweet axis
  6. Era - Release year era
  7. Gender - Gender lean (Masculine/Feminine/Unisex)

Tile Colors:

Exact match
Partial match or close
No match or missing data
Wrong (Brand/Country only)

Tip: A new puzzle is available daily at midnight (Melbourne, Australia time).
