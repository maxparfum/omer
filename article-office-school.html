BEST OFFICE & SCHOOL FRAGRANCES

1) Afnan Supremacy Collector's Edition

Profile: A spicy-sweet take on Creed Aventus with serious punch.

Why it works: Strong performance, versatile scent that exudes confidence in office and school settings.

2) Goldfield & Banks Pacific Rock Moss

Profile: Fresh, airy, and uplifting blue fragrance.

Why it works: Scent is clean and enjoyable, even though performance is weak, perfectly acceptable in a school or office.

3) Afnan 9PM Rebel

Profile: A mix of Bleu de Chanel, Aventus, and Sauvage.

Why it works: Average but versatile. Sometimes safe and simple is all you need. Solid performance ensures reliability.

4) Prada L’Homme

Profile: Clean laundry vibes. Powdery, floral, and fresh.

Why it works: Extremely professional and inoffensive. Not the longest lasting, but easy to reapply on the go.

5) Mind Games Blockade

Profile: Aventus meets God of Fire.

Why it works: Premium yet safe. Perfect for higher-level office settings where you want sophistication with subtle uniqueness.

6) YSL Myself

Profile: A well-balanced mix of citrus, florals, and light sweetness.

Why it works: Universally likable, effortless, and perfectly suited as a daily scent for work or school.
