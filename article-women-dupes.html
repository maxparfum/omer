1:1 WOMEN'S DUPES

Finding a true one-to-one dupe of a women’s fragrance is rare, but some recent releases come impressively close. Here are three accurate alternatives to popular high-end scents.

Ramsh by Zamiya – Dupe of Vanilla 28

Ramsh by Zamiya is a close match to Vanilla 28, with around 90–95% similarity. It delivers the same sweet, warm vanilla profile, with only a faint synthetic note in the opening that fades quickly. Once it settles, the resemblance to Vanilla 28 is striking, making it a strong alternative at a more affordable price.

Her Confession by Lattafa – Dupe of Blanche Bette

Her Confession by Lattafa is widely regarded as one of the closest dupes to Blanche Bette. It captures the same creamy, floral structure but with a smoother finish and less of the sour tonic nuance found in the original. For many, this makes it not only a convincing clone but also a more wearable version.

Lattafa Badee Al Oud Noble Blush – Dupe of Mind Games Prodigy

Badee Al Oud Noble Blush by Lattafa is considered a strong alternative to Mind Games Prodigy. It offers a creamy rose-forward scent that feels unique in its composition, reminiscent of a floral milkshake. While unusual, it mirrors the profile of Prodigy closely and provides a high-quality dupe experience.

Final Thoughts

For women seeking high-quality dupes that stay true to the originals, these options deliver. Ramsh by Zamiya recreates Vanilla 28 with impressive accuracy, Her Confession by Lattafa smooths out Blanche Bette’s rough edges, and Noble Blush captures the creamy floral profile of Prodigy. All three stand out as reliable 1:1 alternatives at a fraction of the cost.
