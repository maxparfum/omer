BEST DUPES OF 2025

1) Armaf Club de Nuit Intense Man

What it dupes: Creed Aventus

Why it’s great in 2025: Still the smoky pineapple king at a fraction of the price. Birch, citrus, and musk deliver that unmistakable Aventus aura with serious projection.

When to wear: Summer evenings, outdoor events, nights out.

2) Afnan 9PM

What it dupes: Jean Paul Gaultier Ultra Male

Why it’s great in 2025: Sweet apple, vanilla, cinnamon, and amber create a loud, playful trail that grabs compliments without feeling dated.

When to wear: Parties, date nights, summer nightlife.

3) Lattafa Khamrah

What it dupes: Kilian Angels’ Share

Why it’s great in 2025: A warm, boozy cinnamon-vanilla fragrance that perfectly captures the rich cognac-and-dessert vibe of Angels’ Share at a fraction of the price. Sweet, luxurious, and long-lasting.

When to wear: Autumn and winter nights, formal evenings, special occasions.

4) Khadlaj Caffe Latte

What it dupes: Mancera Amore Caffé

Why it’s great in 2025: Opens with a realistic coffee blast softened by caramel and vanilla, then dries into a warm, slightly fruity gourmand that feels niche-quality without the niche price.

When to wear: Chilly mornings, cozy café dates, casual evenings.

5) French Avenue Liquid Brun

What it dupes: PDM Althair

Why it’s great in 2025: Cardamom, vanilla, and amber create a creamy, mouthwatering vibe, luxurious on a budget and wearable on summer nights with light sprays.

When to wear: Dates, late-night gatherings, dressed-up evenings.

6) Lattafa Asad

What it dupes: Dior Sauvage Elixir (powerful aromatic-spice)

Why it’s great in 2025: Bold lavender, spices, and dense woods for a commanding presence that still cuts through humid air, easy on the trigger.

When to wear: Summer nights, events, when you need strong projection.

7) Armaf Club de Nuit Sillage

What it dupes: Creed Silver Mountain Water

Why it’s great in 2025: Fresh, musky, slightly metallic tea-citrus vibe that feels icy-clean and refined, fantastic for hot days.

When to wear: Office in summer, daytime outings, minimalist clean looks.
